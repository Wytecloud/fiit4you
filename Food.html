Meal Plan for Bodybuilding: What to Eat and Avoid

Calorie Needs and Macronutrients

Calories
- During your bulking phase, it is recommended to increase your calorie by 15%
- Calculate your maintence calorie and multiply it by 15% and that should be amount you should intake
- For people who are underweight, it is also recommended to intake 3000+ calories every day to gain weight
Macronutrients
- To gain weight, you must determine your macronutrient ratio
- 30-35% of your calories from protein
- 55-60% of your calories from carbohydrate
- 15-20% of your calories from fat
Foods to Eat
- Meats, Poultry and Fish for more protein
- Dairy Products: Yogurt, Cheese, and Milk
- Fruits: Oranges, Apples, Banana, Berries, Pineapple
- Vegetables: Broccoli, Spinach, Tomatoes, Mushrooms, Peppers
- Seeds and Nuts: Almonds, Walnuts, Sunflower Seeds
Foods to Avoid
- Fast Food: Burgers, French Fries, Onion Rings
- Added Sugar
- Any types of Alcohol
