Abs exercise

Crunches and Planks

Plank Mountain Climbers Sit-ups
Lay on the floor with your elbows under your shoulders,hands flat on the floor and core engaged.keeping your forearms and kness on the floor slowly raise yourself upwards until your vody is in a straight line from your knees to your head. Hold the position for as long as you can. From a plank position bring your right knee under your chest towards your right elbow. Return your right leg back to plank position, now bring your left leg under your chest towards your left elbow. Congrats you`ve completed one rep of Mountain Climbers, now repeat. Lay on a flat surface with knees up feet touching the ground. Proceed to lift your torso upwards using only your core then loweryour body back down.

Legs exercise

Squats and Jumps

Squats Lunges Leg raises
While standing up with your hands streched out infront of you, slowly lower your body by bending your knees till they almost form a 90deg angle, then come back up adn repeat. Alternate your legs, right infront left towards the back of you, then right behind you and left leg infront of you Lay flat on a safe surface, legs together slowly lift your leg upwards while your torso remains flat on the surface, go as for as you can then hold for a few seconds then release bringing your legs back down to starting position.(this also works your abs)
